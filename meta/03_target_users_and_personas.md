### Target Users & Personas

#### 1. Primary Personas

##### 1.1 Vibe Coder (Solo Builder)

* **Profile**

  * Single developer/founder.
  * Uses tools like Cursor, v0, Cline, Bolt, Lovable to ship quickly.
* **Goals**

  * Turn ideas into working prototypes fast.
  * Keep track of context without writing "boring docs".
* **Pain**

  * Context lives in their head and random chats.
  * Hard to revisit or scale projects later.
* **How Context Ark helps**

  * Converts their brain dump into a structured spec pack.
  * Makes it easy to spin up new AI dev sessions with the same kernel.

---

##### 1.2 Pro Builder / Indie Studio

* **Profile**

  * Small team or indie studio shipping multiple AI/web/mobile projects.
  * Juggles 3â€“20 active projects.
* **Goals**

  * Standardize how projects are documented.
  * Onboard collaborators quickly.
* **Pain**

  * Inconsistent docs per project; spec quality depends on who wrote it.
  * Hard to enforce best practices across multiple clients / repos.
* **How Context Ark helps**

  * Provides a repeatable intake + spec system.
  * Doc pack doubles as:

    * onboarding docs
    * AI agent context
    * client-facing documentation.

---

##### 1.3 Agency / Consultancy

* **Profile**

  * Agency building products, automation, AI workflows for clients.
* **Goals**

  * Scale to many clients without losing quality.
  * Sell "discovery + spec" as a billable unit.
* **Pain**

  * Discovery is unstructured, time-consuming, and hard to standardize.
  * Documentation often lags behind implementation.
* **How Context Ark helps**

  * Offers a standardized "discovery + spec pack" product.
  * Can be white-labelled as the agency's own process.

---

##### 1.4 Internal Product / Platform Teams

* **Profile**

  * Teams inside larger orgs (product managers, solution architects, platform teams).
* **Goals**

  * Align many stakeholders on what's being built.
  * Use AI tools without violating internal rules or duplicating work.
* **Pain**

  * Many different templates across departments.
  * Context scattered between Jira tickets, Confluence, Slack, etc.
* **How Context Ark helps**

  * Centralizes the project's brain and derived docs.
  * Feeds curated context to various AI/internal tools consistently.

---

#### 2. Secondary Personas

##### 2.1 AI Agent Orchestrator / Ops Engineer

* Uses Ark's doc pack as **source of truth** for:

  * which agents exist
  * what they should do
  * which tools they can call.

##### 2.2 Technical Writer / Documentation Lead

* Uses Ark as a **starting point**:

  * Edits/improves generated docs.
  * Maintains consistency across projects.

---

#### 3. Persona Prioritization for MVP

For the **50-doc MVP**, prioritize:

1. **Vibe Coder / Solo Builder**
2. **Pro Builder / Indie Studio**
3. **Small Agency**

Internal enterprise teams and more complex governance flows come later. The MVP should:

* Feel approachable to a solo builder.
* Be structured enough to scale into studio/agency workflows.
