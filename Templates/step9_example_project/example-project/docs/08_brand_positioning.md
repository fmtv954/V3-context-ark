# 08 – Product Naming, Brand & Positioning

## Working Name

**Quantum Voice AI** – QR-to-Call Lead Agent.

## Tagline Ideas

- "Turn every scan into a conversation."
- "Your always-on voice receptionist for QR leads."

## Brand Voice

- Calm, confident, helpful.
- Feels like a smart assistant, not a robot.
- Explains things in plain language, avoids jargon.

## Positioning Statement

For **small businesses and agents** who rely on leads from offline and
local channels, Quantum Voice AI is a **voice-first lead capture layer**
that turns QR scans into qualified conversations and follow-up tasks,
without hiring a full-time receptionist.
