# 18 – User Journeys & Scenarios

## Persona 1 – Real Estate Agent (Admin)

- Creates a campaign for a specific listing.
- Prints QR on a yard sign and flyers.
- Receives Asana tasks and email summaries when people call.
- Uses summaries to schedule showings and follow up.

## Persona 2 – Buyer (Caller)

- Sees a yard sign while driving by.
- Scans QR and joins call without installing an app.
- Asks questions about the property.
- AI captures their name, contact, budget, and timing.

## Persona 3 – Vibe Coder (Builder)

- Opens this project in Cursor/Windsurf.
- Reads AGENTS.md and docs/.
- Uses platform rules to scaffold the codebase and implement the MVP,
  focusing on delivering the QR-to-call flow first.
